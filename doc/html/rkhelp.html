Syntax

[sysr, V, W] = rk(sys, s0_inp, s0_out, IP)

Inputs

sys

an sss-object containing the LTI system

s0_inp

Expansion points for Input Krylov Subspace

s0_out

Expansion points for Output Krylov Subspace

IP

Inner product (optional)

Outputs

sysr

reduced system

V
W

Projection matrices spanning Krylov subspaces

Examples

No examples

Description

s0 may either be horizontal vectors containing the desired expansion points, e.g. [1 2 3] matches one moment about 1, 2 and 3, respectively. [1+1j 1-1j 5 5 5 5 inf inf] matches one moment about 1+1j, 1-1j, 4 moments about 5 and 2 Ma|rk|ov parameters.

An alternative notation for s0 is a two-row matrix, containing the expansion points in the first row and their multiplicity in the second, e.g. [4 pi inf; 1 20 10] matches one moment about 4, 20 moments about pi and 10 Ma|rk|ov parameters.

To perform one-sided rk, set s0_inp or s0_out to [], respectively.
